immersive Media

An iOS and Android app to engage sports fans with immersive media

This project's goal was to determine the MVP product features that met the stakeholders and end-user needs and would engage fans by using a multinational technology company’s premium video content and clip tagging technology. Despite working with a tight timeline, the project was incredibly successful. This project is currently under NDA.
