Detroit Connect

An easy-to-access portal where users can access real-time information about their truck fleets.

I rewrote the entire CSS front-end and developed a living styleguide for Daimlers Detroit Connect. I have also been the lead UX Designer on many small and larger features for Detroit Connect, including some updates to its iOS app. I worked directly with stakeholders to solve complex problems, improve the experience, and introduce new features with the results of winning more UX and development work for Metal Toad.
