Detroit Connect Direct

DTNA’s largest customers desire an automated way to access vehicle data on an ongoing basis, in a format that is customizable to their needs based on data type and aggregation. Detroit Connect Direct enables DTNA customers to configure their data needs and then receive vehicle data directly into their systems.

Not only will Detroit Connect Direct provide an essential and valuable service to DTNA’s most valuable customers, it will also generate revenue for DTNA through the sale of data tiers at vehicle point-of-sale.
