Househappy

a platform for real estate buyers and sellers to search and discover homes.

Househappy is an end-to-end platform for supporting and navigating home buyers and sellers through the homeownership cycle—from researching and making an offer, to remodeling and selling. Househappy's goal is to simplify property search and make the home ownership process easily accessible to everyone.
