About Me

I transform complex challenges into elegant user experiences as a Principal UX Designer with front-end development expertise. I bridge business goals, user needs, and technical realities—equally comfortable coding prototypes as presenting strategic designs to executives.

Driven by curiosity, I constantly explore new apps, landscapes, dinner spots, and design systems. My adaptability powers my leadership of cross-functional teams and multi-device experiences.
