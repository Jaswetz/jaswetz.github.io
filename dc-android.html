Detroit Connect Android

The power of Detroit Connect available on Android

This project required me to convert the existing iOS Detroit Connect app into an Android app. Utilizing Android’s Material Design best practices, I made the app recognizable and seamless on Android devices. This project required me to educate the client on the differences between iOS and Android, produce prototypes, work side-by-side with developers, and create animations. I not only translated an iOS app into an Android app- I also improved the usability and design in the process.
