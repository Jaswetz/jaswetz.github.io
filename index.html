About Me

As a Principal UX Designer & Front-End Specialist with over 6 years of experience at Autodesk, I specialize in leading and crafting user-centric solutions that align with both user needs and business goals. My work spans across diverse domains like growth-focused initiatives, architecting complex data management flows, scalable design systems, and cross-platform experiences.

With extensive experience in front-end development, I am uniquely positioned to bridge the gap between strategic design vision and technical implementation. This combination of skills enables me to deliver solutions that are both innovative and technically sound.

Collaboration is at the heart of my approach. I thrive on building strong working relationships and fostering cross-functional teamwork, believing that true innovation emerges from shared ideas and user engagement. My process is deeply rooted in user research and design thinking, ensuring every solution addresses real user needs while driving measurable business impact.

Key areas of expertise:

  • Growth-driven UX strategy
  • Complex information architecture & data visualization
  • Scalable design systems & UI frameworks
  • Cross-platform & device-independent experiences
  • Front-end development collaboration (HTML/CSS/JS)
  • User Research and Design thinking
  • Petting cats and dogs.

Let's connect—I'm eager to discuss UX trends, exchange insights, or explore opportunities for new challenges! Reach out, I'm friendly :)
